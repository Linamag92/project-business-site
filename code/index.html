Orders

The items you add to your Shopping Bag will be kept there for as long as you are logged in. You may purchase up to five items in a single order in your first purchase. All items that display an “Add to Shopping Bag” button may be purchased online, subject to availability. Once you have made your selection, please click the “Add to Shopping Bag” button to place the product in your shopping bag. When you are ready to finalise your order, please click the “Checkout” button: you will then be guided through the order process. You may remove products from your shopping bag by clicking on "Delete”. To complete your purchase you will need to create an account or log in to your existing account. When you have completely reviewed your order, click on the "Confirm and place order" button on the Website to place your order.

Shipping

Shipping options depend on your delivery address. The estimated delivery time and shipping fees are indicated when the billing address is entered. Please note that a personalised creation (e.g. engraving or bracelet adjustment) will require a longer delivery time. You may also contact Cartier Client Services. For standard delivery, your creation should be delivered within three business days (except Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays). For an express delivery, your creation should be delivered within two business days (except Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays). Delivery may take longer if you have requested engraving or bracelet adjustment. For further information, please check our conditions of sales. If you require a faster method of delivery, please contact one of our Cartier Ambassadors who will do their best to accommodate your request.

Returns and exchange

You will need to contact Cartier Client Services within 14 days. Cartier Client Services will issue a Return Authorisation Number to you by email. Upon receipt of this number you have 14 days to return or exchange your purchase.
